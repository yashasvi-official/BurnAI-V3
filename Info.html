Setup Your Virtual Trainer

Follow these simple steps to begin your fitness journey with BurnAI

01

Click on Start Button

02

Allow Camera Access

Allow camera access if you see a popUp

03

Wait for the Camera to Start

Please wait a moment for the camera to load

04

Calibration

Stand up and keep your right arm straight, ensuring that your face and body remain in view of the camera.

The webapp will show calibration progress at the bottom with blue text.

You will be notified with a voice command once the calibration completes.

05

Basic Instructions

  • Reps will be counted if straighten your Right Arm and then curl it
  • Keep your elbows close to your waist to avoid error
  • An auditory feedback will remind you of errors during session
06

Getting Feedback

Click on the End button to finish your session and view detailed visual analysis of your workout
