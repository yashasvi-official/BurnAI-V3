The Project

BurnAI is more than just a fitness tracker—it's your personal guide to safe and effective home workouts. Powered by advanced computer vision, it ensures you maintain the right posture, reducing the risk of injuries while maximizing results. This project was born from a personal fitness journey—shedding 15kg and realizing firsthand the challenges of working out without guidance. The struggle of maintaining proper form, avoiding injuries, and staying consistent inspired the creation of burnAI. It's designed to empower anyone, regardless of experience, to exercise confidently and safely from home. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned fitness enthusiast, burnAI is here to support your journey, helping you train smarter and achieve your goals with precision and ease.
