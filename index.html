Problem Statement

Gym memberships can be frustrating—they're costly, and personal training comes with extra fees. While home workouts save both money and time, improper form can lead to serious injuries.

To address this, we developed BurnAI, a computer vision-powered web app that monitors your bicep curls in real time and provides instant feedback for a safer workout experience.
